Membership & Cancellation Terms

The following terms govern your VFIT membership, including session attendance, cancellation obligations, and membership termination. By agreeing below, you confirm you have read and understood the terms applicable to your chosen membership type.

1. Flexible Membership — $70 per session

The Flexible Membership operates on a no lock-in basis. The following terms apply:

a. Session cancellations: A minimum of 4 weeks written notice is required to cancel or reschedule a scheduled session. Where notice of less than 4 weeks is provided, the full session fee of $70 will be charged regardless of attendance.

b. No-shows: Failure to attend a booked session without any prior notice will result in the full session fee being charged.

c. Membership termination: You may terminate your Flexible Membership at any time without penalty. There is no minimum commitment period. Termination takes effect immediately upon written notice to Georgie, and no further sessions will be charged from that date.

2. Signature Membership — $60 per session

The Signature Membership is subject to a minimum 3-month commitment. The following terms apply:

a. Session cancellations: A minimum of 1 week's written notice is required to cancel or reschedule a scheduled session. Where less than 1 week's notice is provided, the full session fee of $60 will be charged.

b. No-shows: Failure to attend a booked session without any prior notice will result in the full session fee being charged.

c. Minimum term: You are committed to a minimum period of 3 months from your membership start date. Sessions will continue to be billed throughout this period regardless of attendance.

d. Membership termination: Following the completion of your 3-month minimum term, you may terminate this membership by providing a minimum of 1 month's written notice to Georgie. Early termination within the minimum term is not permitted, except in the case of a medical condition supported by a valid doctor's certificate.

3. VIP Membership — $190 per session

The VIP Membership is a premium private training arrangement subject to a 3-month commitment. The following terms apply:

a. Session cancellations: A minimum of 1 week's written notice is required to cancel or reschedule a scheduled session. Where less than 1 week's notice is provided, the full session fee of $190 will be charged.

b. No-shows: Failure to attend a booked session without any prior notice will result in the full session fee being charged.

c. Minimum term: You are committed to a minimum period of 3 months from your membership start date. Sessions will continue to be billed throughout this period regardless of attendance.

d. Membership termination: Following the completion of your 3-month minimum term, you may terminate this membership by providing a minimum of 1 month's written notice to Georgie. Early termination within the minimum term is not permitted, except in the case of a medical condition supported by a valid doctor's certificate.

4. General Terms — All Memberships

a. All cancellation and termination notices must be submitted in writing directly to Georgie Valdal.

b. VFIT reserves the right to review and update membership terms with reasonable notice provided to all members.

c. Sessions are non-transferable and cannot be shared with another person.

d. This agreement is governed by the laws of Australia and subject to the jurisdiction of the courts of Queensland.
